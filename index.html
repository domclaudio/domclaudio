About

I write about people deciding who to be, often at a cost.

I came to screenwriting sideways. I woke up from a coma in 2010 with a secret to tell. I picked up a pen in the hospital and haven't put it down since.

When I lost my voice, I used Morgan Freeman's. I haven't quite given it back.

B.A. in Literature, Ramapo College. Living in a simulation.
