A MeUndies Campaign
Short-Form Social Series  ·  TikTok  ·  Instagram Reels
The Concept

MeUndies members unlock a way of being that most people don't understand because they've never felt the long-term effects of being consistently comfortable. Their most precious cargo is handled. And it shows.

One character, Frankie, experiences the same everyday scenario twice. Version A: uncomfortable, fidgeting, reactive. Version B: settled, smooth, pulse at 60 bpm. The product is never the punchline. It's the invisible reason everything goes right.

About

I'm Dom Claudio — a writer, content strategist, and screenwriter based in Los Angeles. My background spans copywriting, brand storytelling, and community-facing content, with experience across social media marketing, real estate, and creative development.

I studied literature at Ramapo College of New Jersey. I've worked with Greystar, Sares Regis, RW Selby, and GHP Management across 15+ communities in Los Angeles and Ventura counties. I'm currently developing several projects for screen and building a portfolio for brands that need sharp, human writing.
